2022-23 Family Directory

This directory contains the contact information of families who have opted in to share their contact information with other families of the school. The purpose of this directory is to allow students and parents to communicate regarding class issues, regional meetups, and other OHS based activities. The family directory should not be used for solicitations of any kind. If you wish to send an announcement, please email ohsstudentlife@stanford.edu for your announcement to be placed in the OHS Community Bulletin located in the Pixel Weekly. Misuse of the family directory should be reported to the Director of Student Life and Community.
